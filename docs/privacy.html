Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026
✦ Tab Tidy does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What we access

Tab Tidy requests the following browser permissions:


  • tabs — to read the URLs of your open tabs so they can be grouped by category. Tab URLs are processed locally in your browser and never sent anywhere.
  • tabGroups — to create, name, and color-code tab groups within your browser window.
  • storage — to save your custom category settings (names, colors, URL patterns) locally using Chrome's built-in sync storage. This data stays within your Google account's sync and is never accessed by us.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track browsing history
  • We do not send any data to external servers
  • We do not use analytics or telemetry
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We have no backend servers or databases

Data storage

Your category preferences are stored using chrome.storage.sync, which is a browser-native API. This syncs your settings across your own devices via your browser's built-in sync — it is governed by Google's privacy policy, not ours.

Open source

Tab Tidy is fully open source. You can inspect every line of code at github.com/ekyeremeh7/tab-tidy.
